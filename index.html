Who am I? Hello! My name is Calvin Hunter. I graduated from University of California, Santa Cruz for Computer Science: Game Design. I enjoy doing outdoor activities such as hiking and snowboarding as well as playing sports such as baseball and volleyball. What do you want to accomplish? I want to be able to create a game with a community. I want people to talk about my work, analyze it, criticize it, and love it. I would also like to create something that I enjoy playing as well, I'm a very picky gamer, so I'm hoping I could appeal to the masses while appealing to myself.
